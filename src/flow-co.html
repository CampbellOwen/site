Brand Design

The Client

Flow Coworking is a hotdesk and office rental company based in Vancouver.

The Problem

As a new company, they didn't have a brand or website but needed to show potential renters images of the offices, ammenities details, and location information, through a frictionless expereience that would reflect well on the business.

The Outcome

Defined a brand grounded in the desire to find focus in the separation from work and homelife, and built a Squarespace website for the business within 1 day.
