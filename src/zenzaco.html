Zenzaco Towing

Emergency towing service in the Chilcotin-Caribou region of Northern BC.

Project Scope

Brand Design

The Client

Named for a creek in the region, Zenzaco Towing is an emergency towing service in the Chilcotin-Caribou Region.

The Problem

As a new company, they didn't have a brand. They were interested in a brand identity that conveyed the nature of their services at a glance, and emphasized their speedy response.

The Goal

Design a logo for the business within 4 hours.
